Our Approach to Recovery

Holistic Healing Philosophy

We believe that true recovery addresses the whole person. Our holistic approach integrates multiple dimensions of wellness:

Physical Wellness

Access to nutritious food through our Abundant Harvest program, connections to healthcare resources, and support for developing healthy lifestyle habits.

Mental & Emotional Wellness

Trauma-informed support, peer connection, stress management techniques, and referrals to mental health professionals as needed.

Social Wellness

Building healthy relationships, community integration, and developing support networks that sustain recovery beyond our programs.

Spiritual Wellness

Exploring meaning, purpose, and connection through practices that resonate with each individual's beliefs and values.

Trauma-Informed Care Principles

Our trauma-informed approach is guided by six key principles:

  • Safety: Creating physical and emotional safety in all interactions
  • Trustworthiness: Building trust through transparency and consistency
  • Peer Support: Leveraging the healing power of shared experience
  • Collaboration: Partnering with individuals in their own recovery
  • Empowerment: Recognizing strengths and building on existing resilience
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Honoring diverse backgrounds and experiences
